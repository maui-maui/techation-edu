  This assignment is based on creativity. Its a toolkit this can be as 
simple or as complex as you want and you can make it do many useful 
things. For one you can make a tool box that can do some of the 
following things:

-Give date/time

-A todo list

-A notebook to add quick notes

-An app opener (open firefox or chrome if your not sure what app to 
choose) **Make this more complex if you want by adding different pages 
you can open in the tool kit**

-A timer

-A live clock

-An alarmclock

-A reminder 


  These should be plenty of ideas for you to get started with. You can 
submit to us on our server or you can share it with us on our facebook 
once its ready... (As of Apr. 13th, 17)
